FAQs

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about our documentation and legal services in Dwarka, Delhi.

What documents are required for Aadhaar update?
+

For Aadhaar update you typically need a valid proof of identity (passport, voter ID, PAN card), proof of address (bank statement, utility bill, rent agreement), and proof of date of birth (birth certificate, school certificate). Our team will guide you on the exact documents based on the type of update you need.

How long does it take to process a PAN card?
+

A new PAN card application typically takes 7–15 working days for delivery after submission. We handle the complete online application process for you — from form filling to submission. You'll receive a tracking number to monitor your application status.

Can I get an Income or Domicile Certificate here?
+

Yes! We assist with Income Certificate, Domicile Certificate, SC/ST/OBC Certificate, EWS Certificate, and many more. These are processed through the Delhi government portal and usually take 7–21 working days depending on the type of certificate and government processing time.

Do you help with Passport applications?
+

Yes, we assist with fresh Passport applications, renewals, and Tatkal Passport services. We help you fill and submit the online form, prepare the correct set of documents, and guide you for the appointment at the Passport Seva Kendra. The actual passport issuance is done by the Government of India.

What is a Gazette notification and when do I need it?
+

A Gazette notification (also called Gazette Work) is an official government publication used to record legal changes — most commonly for name changes. You need it when you change your name after marriage, for correcting name discrepancies across documents, or for any official name change. We handle the complete gazette process end-to-end.

How do I get a Rent Agreement made?
+

Bring the landlord's and tenant's Aadhaar cards, photos, and the agreed terms (rent amount, duration, etc.). We draft a legally valid rent agreement, get it printed on stamp paper of the appropriate value, and have it notarized or registered as required. Most rent agreements are ready within the same day.

What services do you offer for property matters?
+

We assist with Property Registration, Mutation, NOC (No Objection Certificate), Stamping, DDA Flat related paperwork, and Rent Agreements. Our experienced team and advocate support ensures your property documents are legally sound and properly processed with the relevant government departments.

How much do your services cost?
+

Our pricing is transparent and competitive with no hidden charges. Service fees vary based on the type of document and complexity of work involved. We provide a clear quote before starting any work. Contact us on WhatsApp or call for a free price estimate on any specific service.

Do you provide Legal Heir Certificate assistance?
+

Yes. A Legal Heir Certificate is required to claim the assets and benefits of a deceased person. We assist you in preparing the application, affidavit, and all supporting documents required by the SDM Office or court. With our advocate support, this process becomes significantly easier and faster.
